Who is RUSHIL?

Bridging Two Worlds Of Music.

RUSHIL is a singer-songwriter, performer, producer, audio engineer and DJ from New Jersey. At just 22 years of age, he has already begun to build a global audience and differentiates himself through his diverse skillset, “genre less” versatility, emotionally invoking soundscape, multilingual capabilities, and above all, his distinct and soulful voice suited for a wide range of music styles. Having cultural roots in India but being born & raised in the US, he represents two worlds of music. RUSHIL started his musical journey at age 6 with Indian Classical music, which served as the foundation for his expansion and success in the Pop, RnB, Bollywood, and Independent Indian music spaces.

In 2024, RUSHIL is bringing a new artistic vision to fruition with his upcoming studio originals and collaborations. With a dynamic voice & persona, new creative direction, and a growing global presence, RUSHIL is on his way to being the first global Indian-American male pop star.
